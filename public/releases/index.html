Current release

Keystone thesis build: benchmarking workflows, quantum integration, hybrid encryption, and research reporting.

Thesis build

  • Cryptographic benchmarking workflows for post-quantum and classical schemes.
  • Quantum execution integration for Shor and Grover demonstrations.
  • Hybrid encryption demonstration using ML-KEM, AES-GCM, and ML-DSA.
  • Research export and reporting surfaces for reviewing experiment evidence.

Future directions

  • Linux timing improvements for broader benchmarking environments.
  • Additional quantum backends and provider adapters.
  • Expanded comparison tooling for algorithm families and parameter sets.
  • More portable reporting around backend noise, circuit depth, and execution context.
