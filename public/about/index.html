About Keystone

Keystone is a post-quantum cryptography benchmarking platform developed from a bachelor thesis on cryptographic schemes applicable to post-quantum cryptography.

Audience

Built for cryptographic researchers, security analysts, students, and educators who need explainable evaluation workflows.

Research-first, practical by design

The application connects benchmark execution, quantum resource analysis, and hybrid cryptographic demonstrations in one inspection-oriented workflow.

Its purpose is to make post-quantum tradeoffs easier to study: what algorithms are measured, what hardware and libraries are involved, what quantum assumptions are being demonstrated, and where limitations remain.
